    Skoda New Kodiaq Summary

    Price

    The Skoda New Kodiaq prices are expected to range between Rs. 45.00 Lakh - Rs. 55.00 Lakh, depending on the variant selected.

    When will the new Skoda Kodiaq was unveiled? 

    The new Skoda Kodiaq was unveiled on 4 October 2023.

    What variants does it get?

    No variant details of the Skoda Kodiaq have been revealed. However, we know that there will be a standard version, sportline version, L & K version and in the future a Kodiaq RS.

    What features are available in the Skoda Kodiaq?

    On the outside, the Kodiaq gets a silhouette similar to that of the current car but with a new design for the headlamps, LED DRLs and tail lamps. Inside, Skoda has fitted the Kodiaq with dual digital displays, a two-spoke steering wheel, climate control system, power seats, ambient lighting and leatherette upholstery. It also gets ADAS, a 360-degree camera, and a plethora of storage spaces as a part of the deal.

    What are the engine, performance and specifications of the Skoda Kodiaq?

    Globally, the Skoda Kodiaq can be had with four-cylinder petrol and diesel engines which are unchanged for this next-generation vehicle. Skoda for the first time is also offering mild-hybrid technology and plugin hybrid technology of which the latter is capable of 100km pure electric range and DC fast charging.

    Is the Skoda Kodiaq a safe car?

    The upcoming Skoda Kodiaq has not been tested for an NCAP rating as yet.

    What are the rivals to the Skoda Kodiaq?

    The direct alternatives to the Skoda Kodiaq are the Volkswagen Tiguan, Toyota Fortuner, Hyundai Tucson, and the MG Gloster.
